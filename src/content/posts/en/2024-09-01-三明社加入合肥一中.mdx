# 🎉 Big News｜Triluminous Club Officially Joins Hefei No.1 High School!

## 🗓️ September 11, 2024  
Triluminous Club has officially been approved as a registered student club under the Grade Division of Hefei No.1 High School! We sincerely thank the school for its trust and support.

Triluminous Club will continue its mission to promote traditional Chinese culture through engaging seal engraving activities, helping more students appreciate the beauty and joy of this art.

We look forward to Triluminous Club’s growth and a bright, creative future!
