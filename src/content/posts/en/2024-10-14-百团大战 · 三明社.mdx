# 💥 Club Fair Highlight · Triluminous Club Is Recruiting!

> 📍 The Club Fair is the **only official way** to join a club at Hefei No.1 High School.  
> 📣 Want to try something hands-on, artistic, and uniquely Chinese? Come check out **Triluminous Club**!

---

## 🧧 What You Get When You Join:

### 🛠️ Tools Included  
Get a **carving knife and two seal stones** the moment you join!  
You’ll be fully equipped to begin your seal engraving journey.

### 📘 Custom-Made Textbook  
Our club has its own **student-friendly guide**, easy to follow for complete beginners.

### 🧑‍🏫 Personalized Teaching  
One-on-one guidance from the club leader, with different difficulty levels tailored to your needs.

### 🎨 Art Festival Showcase  
Showcase your masterpiece at the **Hefei No.1 Cultural & Arts Festival** and share your creativity with the whole school!

---

## ❓ Triluminous Q&A: What New Members Want to Know

### 💬 Q: What does Triluminous Club actually do?
- **Official version:**  
  We promote traditional Chinese culture through the ancient art of seal engraving, helping students grow in cultural appreciation and personal artistry.

- **Student version:**  
  We play with rocks. But like… professionally. 😎

---

### 💬 Q: I've never done this before. Can I still join?
Of course!  
With our beginner-friendly textbook, structured lessons, and one-on-one support,  
**you’ll be carving like a pro in no time.**

---

### 💬 Q: How long does it take to finish a seal?
Depends on the design, but most students finish their **first seal in just two sessions**.  
Simple ones are quick and super fun!

---

### 💬 Q: Can I take my seal home after carving it?
Absolutely!  
You get two free stones when you join—and whatever you carve, you keep!  
Take your creation home and show it off.
